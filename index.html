Hi, I'm Emily Deadman

Full Stack Software Engineer & Artist

Don't let the last name scare you! Art has been my lifelong passion and led me to major in graphic design in college, however I recently took a turn in my career goals to focus on software engineering. I have previously worked in early education as an assistant teacher to elementary school children as well as children with disabilities. I am passionate about and look forward to using my patience, past graphic design experience and current software engineering experience to enter the tech industry with a focus on front-end development. My ultimate goal is to develop technology in creative ways to strengthen underprivileged communities and to help the environment.

In my spare time I enjoy trying new food and going to the beach. Although sometimes intimidating, fashion design has always interested me.
